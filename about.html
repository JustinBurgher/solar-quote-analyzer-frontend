About Solar Verify

Protecting UK homeowners from solar scams and overpriced quotes since 2024

Our Story

Solar Verify was born from a simple frustration: too many UK homeowners are getting ripped off by solar companies. After seeing countless friends and family members pay thousands more than they should for solar installations, we decided enough was enough.

The solar industry is booming, but with that growth has come an influx of companies taking advantage of consumers who don't know how to evaluate solar quotes. Overpriced systems, low-quality components, and misleading claims have become far too common.

We created Solar Verify to level the playing field. Our mission is simple: give every UK homeowner the tools and knowledge they need to make informed decisions about solar installations and avoid getting ripped off.
