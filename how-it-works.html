Our Grading System

We use a simple A-F grading system to help you quickly understand the quality of your solar quote:

A
Excellent value, high quality components, competitive pricing
B
Good value, quality components, fair pricing
C
Average value, acceptable quality, market-rate pricing
D
Below average, overpriced or quality concerns
F
Poor value, significant overpricing or quality issues

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is your analysis?
Our analysis is based on real-time UK market data, updated daily. We track pricing from hundreds of installers and maintain a comprehensive database of component specifications and performance ratings.
Do you work with solar installers?
No, we are completely independent. We don't receive commissions or payments from installers, which allows us to provide unbiased analysis focused solely on your interests.
What if my quote gets a poor grade?
A poor grade doesn't necessarily mean you should reject the quote entirely. We provide detailed explanations of issues and suggestions for improvement, which you can use to negotiate better terms.
Can you analyze quotes from any installer?
Yes, our analysis works for quotes from any UK solar installer. We evaluate the fundamentals: pricing, components, and value proposition, regardless of who provided the quote.
How long does the analysis take?
Basic analysis is instant - you'll see results within seconds of submitting your quote details. Premium analysis with detailed reports typically takes 24-48 hours.
What's included in premium analysis?
Premium analysis includes detailed component breakdowns, contract review, installer background checks, negotiation strategies, and personalized recommendations based on your specific situation.
